Mexico, officially the United Mexican States, is a country in the southern portion of North America. It is bordered to the north by the United States; to the south and west by the Pacific Ocean; to the southeast by Guatemala, Belize, and the Caribbean Sea; and to the east by the Gulf of Mexico.

Tips of visiting Mexico

  • Choose your destination carefully
  • Register your trip with the State Department’s STEP (Smart Traveler Enrollment Program)
  • If you’re an extremely cautious traveler, consider an all-inclusive resort or a cruise
  • Think twice about renting a car
  • Monitor what you eat and drink
  • Get vaccinated and consider bringing medication
