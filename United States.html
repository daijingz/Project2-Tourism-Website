The U.S. is a country of 50 states covering a vast swath of North America, with Alaska and Hawaii. Midwestern metropolis Chicago is known for influential architecture and on the west coast, Los Angeles' Hollywood is famed for filmmaking.

Tips of visiting USA

  • Be prepared to pay more than you think (most prices are listed excluding tax)
  • 911 is the emergency number in the US (call 911 when meet fire, criminals and other emergency situations)
  • The US spans six time zones; make sure you know which time zone your destination is in for your flights and airport transfers.
  • Credit cards and bank cards are usually the preferred mode of payment.
  • The US is one of only a few countries to still use the Imperial system. So, think of height in feet, distance in miles, temperature in Fahrenheit, and weight in pounds.
